FAQ

  1. Where is the event taking place?

    The event is taking place at Kazakh-British Technical University, located at Tole-bi street 59, Almaty. The entrance is from Abylaykhan street side.

  2. What is the ICPC?

    The International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) is an annual multi-tiered competitive programming competition among the universities of the world.

  3. Who are eligible to participate?

    The entire competition will be held both onsite and online, and everyone is welcome to participate!

  4. How many people should be in a team?

    Teams should be formed out of a maximum of three people.

  5. What kind of problems will we solve?

    The problems that will be solved during the competition are similar to those found on Codeforces. To get an idea, have a look at this: https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/4/A

  6. Who have prepared the problems?

    All the problems have been prepared by KBTU students and alumni.
